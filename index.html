Fragile X syndrome (FXS)

FXS is a type of Neurodevelopmental Disorders just like autism and Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder(ADHD) and more.

FXS is is a genetic disorder which caused by changes in a gene called Fragile X Messenger Ribonucleoprotein 1 (FMR1).

FXS affects both males and females. However, females often have milder symptoms than males.FXS is named fragile X syndrome because, when looked at through a microscope, part of the X chromosome looks “broken” or “fragile.”

Symptoms

Intelligence issues

  • Low intelligence quotient (IQ). Their IQ scores decrease with age.
  • Learning disabilities.

Mental health problems

  • Anxiety.
  • Depression.

Behavior

  • Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD).
  • Flapping or biting their hands.

Treatment(FXS)

  1. Medication
  2. behavioral therapy
  3. Special Education
